About Urban Yards

Local property care with a steady hand.

Urban Yards Groundskeeping exists for properties that need consistent upkeep, clear communication, and practical landscape choices that look good through the season.

What Matters

Reliable work, clean edges, thoughtful planting, and property support that makes first impressions easier to maintain.

The Approach

Built for useful, visible progress.

Urban Yards is focused on dependable groundskeeping, landscape improvements, and ecological property care for Beaverton, Portland, and Vancouver. The work is meant to be practical first: cleaner entries, healthier beds, better curb appeal, and outdoor spaces that feel intentionally maintained.

That can mean recurring mowing and edging, a seasonal cleanup, a mulch refresh, a small planting upgrade, or support around shared spaces that get heavy daily use.
