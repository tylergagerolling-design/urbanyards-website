        Property Management Care

        Apartment Turnover Cleaning & Light Repair Support

        Move-out cleaning, debris removal, touch-up tasks, and small repair support to help rental units and shared spaces feel ready for the next resident.

        Urban Yards helps property managers and small multifamily owners with practical turnover support between residents. Services can include move-out cleaning, trash and debris removal, wiping surfaces, cleaning entry areas, light patching, touch-up paint, minor hardware fixes, caulking touch-ups, bulb replacement, and general reset tasks that help a unit or common area feel clean, presentable, and ready for the next step.

        • Move-out cleaning and reset tasks
        • Trash, junk, and debris removal
        • Entryway, hallway, and common-area cleaning
        • Baseboard, cabinet, and surface wipe-downs
        • Light wall patching and touch-up paint
        • Minor hardware tightening or replacement
        • Caulking touch-ups where appropriate
        • Light bulb replacement and small detail fixes
