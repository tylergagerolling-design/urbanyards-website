The Feral Grounds

A future community project for native plants, small habitat corridors, and rehabilitating overlooked urban spaces into living ground again.

The Idea

Community care for city soil.

The Feral Grounds will focus on practical native rehabilitation: replacing neglected patches with resilient plantings, supporting pollinators, and helping neighbors imagine wilder, healthier urban edges.

Early Focus

Built around small, useful interventions.

01

Native Plant Rehab

Guidance and small installs that favor regional plants, seasonal texture, and habitat value.

02

Urban Habitat Patches

Pollinator pockets, curbside edges, shared yards, and overlooked corners brought back into use.

03

Community Stewardship

Volunteer-ready projects, simple care plans, and visible progress neighbors can participate in.
